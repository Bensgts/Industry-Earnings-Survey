Hours

Question 1

What are your ordinary standard weekly hours?

Definition

  • Ordinary time weekly standard hours are those which the occupant of the job is normally paid for in a week.

For employees who do not have standard hours (e.g. casuals) please calculate the average of the ordinary time hours paid for in the last 4 weeks.

Report weekly
For example, 38:30 for 38 hours and 30 minutes.

Error: Enter your hours in HH:MM format, for example 38:30.

OR (if decimal)

Decimal hours alternative
To 2 decimal places, for example 38.50.

Error: Enter a number with up to 2 decimal places, for example 38.50.
