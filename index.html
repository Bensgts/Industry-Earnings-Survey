Introduction

About this survey

Purpose of survey

Thank you for participating in the Industry Earnings Survey. This short survey collects essential information about earnings within your industry to support the development of accurate, up-to-date national statistics. Your responses help build a clearer picture of economic conditions across Australian employment and contribute directly to evidence-based policy and planning.

What you'll need

To complete this survey, please have the following information ready:

  • Ordinary hours worked
  • Hourly or annual ordinary pay rate

The survey takes only a few minutes to complete.

Confidentiality

Your completed form and personal information are confidential and remain private.
